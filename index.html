STUDENTS AGAINST PERIOD POVERTY

We are an organization started by high schoolers that makes menstrual products readily available for struggling members in our community.

ABOUT CONTACT US

OUR STORY

OUR STORY: It all started with a question: how do women who are struggling financially deal with their periods? Those without access to the "luxury item" that is female sanitary products are often forced to resort to using toilet paper, reused cloth, or washing their undergarments by hand. We got together to come up with the solution. Founded in 2020 during the COVID-10 pandemic, we are a tight-knit group of students dedicated to making essential menstrual products readily available for anyone who needs them.
